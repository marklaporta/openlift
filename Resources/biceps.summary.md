# Biceps Training Tips (Hypertrophy) Summary

## Best Exercises to Model
- Barbell curl
- Dumbbell curl
- Hammer curl
- Preacher curl
- Spider curl
- Incline dumbbell curl

## Programming Notes
- Core movement buckets: standard curl, neutral-grip curl, lengthened-position curl, peak-contraction curl.
- Back training contributes indirect biceps stimulus.
- Frequency is often higher with smaller per-session dose.
