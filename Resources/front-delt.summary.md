# Front Delt Training Tips (Hypertrophy) Summary

## Best Exercises to Model
- Seated/standing overhead press
- Machine shoulder press
- Dumbbell front raise
- Cable front raise

## Programming Notes
- Core movement buckets: shoulder press and front raise.
- Many trainees need less direct front-delt isolation because pressing for chest already loads front delts.
- Keep direct volume configurable and potentially low by default.
