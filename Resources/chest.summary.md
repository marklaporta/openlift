# Chest Training Tips (Hypertrophy) Summary

## Best Exercises to Model
- Flat barbell bench press (medium/wide grip)
- Flat dumbbell press
- Incline dumbbell or barbell press
- Machine chest press
- Cable fly / pec-deck fly

## Programming Notes
- Core movement buckets: horizontal press, incline press, chest fly.
- Typical weekly volume is moderate and can be progressed over time.
- Full ROM and control in the stretched position are emphasized.
