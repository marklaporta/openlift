# Calf Training Tips (Hypertrophy) Summary

## Best Exercises to Model
- Standing calf raise
- Seated calf raise
- Leg press calf raise
- Deficit calf raise variants
- Single-leg calf raise

## Programming Notes
- Core movement buckets: straight-leg and bent-knee plantar flexion.
- Full stretch at bottom and strong peak contraction are highlighted.
- Higher-rep and moderate-rep ranges are both commonly used.
