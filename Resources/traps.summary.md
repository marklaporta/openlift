# Trap Training Tips (Hypertrophy) Summary

## Best Exercises to Model
- Barbell shrug
- Dumbbell shrug
- Cable shrug
- Bent-over shrug variant
- Single-arm cable side shrug

## Programming Notes
- Core movement bucket: shrug/elevation patterns with slight torso-angle variations.
- Traps often receive substantial indirect stimulus from pulling and deadlift patterns.
- Direct trap work can be optional or lower priority depending on user goals.
