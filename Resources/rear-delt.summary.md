# Rear Delt Training Tips (Hypertrophy) Summary

## Best Exercises to Model
- Bent-over rear delt raise
- Cable rear delt raise (cross-body or bilateral)
- Face pull
- Reverse pec-deck

## Programming Notes
- Core movement buckets: rear-delt raise and face-pull style pull-apart.
- Often trained at relatively high frequency with low fatigue per set.
- Back work provides partial overlap but direct rear-delt work is still useful.
